## Bot Detection Explained

Our AI analyzes multiple signals to detect fake engagement:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Engagement Velocity Score">
    Analyzes the **speed** of likes, comments, and shares. Bot-driven content often shows:
    - Sudden spikes in engagement
    - Unnatural growth patterns
    - Suspicious velocity changes
    
    **Lower score = more authentic**
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Comment Quality Score">
    Evaluates **comment authenticity**:
    - Generic/spammy comments
    - Repetitive patterns
    - Bot-like language
    - Profile quality of commenters
    
    **Lower score = higher quality comments**
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Timing Pattern Score">
    Detects **suspicious timing**:
    - Engagement clusters (too many at once)
    - 24/7 engagement (no organic dips)
    - Round-number milestones
    
    **Lower score = more organic timing**
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Ratio Analysis Score">
    Compares engagement **ratios**:
    - Views to likes ratio
    - Likes to comments ratio
    - Shares to views ratio
    
    **Unusual ratios indicate bot activity**
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
  **Overall bot confidence** ranges from 0.0 (authentic) to 1.0 (likely botted). Scores above 0.7 indicate high bot probability.
</Note>
